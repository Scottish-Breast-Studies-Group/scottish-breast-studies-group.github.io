## Mailing List

The Scottish Breast Studies Group maintains a mailing list to inform members of collaborative research opportunities, upcoming webinars, clinical trials, and meetings.

### Information Provided

Members receive updates on:
- New multi-centre study opportunities
- Webinar schedules and registration
- Group meeting dates and programmes
- Clinical trial recruitment information

### Eligibility

The network is open to medical professionals, clinical researchers, and scientists involved in breast cancer care and research in Scotland.

### Data Protection

Email addresses are used solely for SBSG communications and are not shared with third parties. Members may unsubscribe at any time. The group complies with GDPR and NHS Scotland data protection standards.

For further information, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).
